Common questions

Is this really free?

Yes. The leak estimator and the manual audit request are both free. We do not charge for scoping or for the initial review document.

How long does the audit take?

The scoped audit document typically arrives within 1–2 business days after you submit your brief. Usually faster.

Do I need to have exact data?

No. The estimator works with your best guesses. Even rough numbers reveal the order of magnitude of the problem — and that is usually enough to decide whether to go further.

What if I am not ready to commit?

That is fine. The audit is designed to help you decide, not to lock you in. Many clients start with the map and engage 30–60 days later when the timing is right.
