The revenue leak is usually not traffic.

Most service businesses do not need another pretty website first. They need the chain from inquiry to booked call, proposal, and follow-up to behave like one system.

Slow first response

Leads arrive from forms, ads, missed calls, WhatsApp, or inboxes. Nobody answers fast enough, so intent cools down before a human ever sees it.

Broken handoffs

Marketing, admin, intake, and sales all see different fragments. Nobody owns the full next step, so the prospect feels ignored even when the team is busy.

No follow-up memory

Unwon opportunities are not truly lost. They are usually just unworked. IXIA builds the follow-up spine that keeps the pipeline alive without nagging chaos.
