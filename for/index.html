What every sector has in common

The intake failure modes change by industry. The underlying problem is always the same: leads cost real money to generate, and the handoff between "interested" and "booked" is broken.

Problem 1

Slow first response

High-consideration buyers contact multiple providers. The first credible reply wins most of the time. If your team takes hours, you are already behind.

Problem 2

No follow-up system

Most service businesses do a single follow-up, if that. Buyers in legal, medical, and immigration categories often need 3–5 touchpoints before deciding. That gap is pure lost revenue.

Problem 3

Unclear ownership

When a lead arrives, everyone assumes someone else is handling it. Clear intake ownership — who responds, in what form, by when — eliminates the most common source of lead loss.
