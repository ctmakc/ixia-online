How it works

Three phases from first conversation to a running intake system.

Map the leak, fix the gaps, hand off and run. Below is exactly what happens in each phase — deliverables, timeline, and who does what — plus a self-serve track if you would rather start the app today.

Phase 1

Map the leak

A clear-eyed diagnosis of where qualified leads fall through today — before anyone touches a tool.

Deliverables

  • 30-minute operator interview
  • Revenue Leak Map with a cash estimate
  • Prioritised fix list ranked by impact

You provide vs IXIA does

  • You provide: access to current forms, inboxes, and call flow.
  • IXIA does: the audit, the leak estimate, and the priority plan.
Phase 2

Fix the gaps

We rebuild what costs the most: response logic, routing, templates, missed-call capture, and follow-up triggers.

Deliverables

  • Instant AI first-response configured to your enquiries
  • Routing rules and response SLAs
  • Missed-call capture and follow-up cadence
  • Embed snippet live on your site

You provide vs IXIA does

  • You provide: brand voice, service lines, and sign-off on copy.
  • IXIA does: build, configure, and test the full intake loop.
Phase 3

Hand off and run

A working system, trained staff, and a written operator playbook — with IXIA on hand as real leads come through.

Deliverables

  • Staff training on the live system
  • Written operator playbook and templates
  • 30-day post-handoff support

You provide vs IXIA does

  • You provide: the team who will run it day to day.
  • IXIA does: training, documentation, and 30 days of support.
Timeline

A realistic timeline

Most engagements reach a first operational fix in 2–5 weeks. Speed depends on how quickly access and sign-offs come back, not on a six-month transformation program.
